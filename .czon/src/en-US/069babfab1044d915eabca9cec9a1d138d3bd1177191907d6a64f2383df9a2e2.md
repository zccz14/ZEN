# Embla Carousel Effect Test

This is a document for testing the Embla carousel effect.

Multiple consecutive images will be displayed as a carousel. Users can switch views by clicking arrows or dragging the images.

However, if text content is inserted in between, it will break the continuity of the carousel, forming a new group of carousels.
